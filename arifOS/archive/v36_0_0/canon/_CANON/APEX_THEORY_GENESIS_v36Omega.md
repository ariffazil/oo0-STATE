## 1. The Three Core Laws (Δ, Ω, Ψ)

APEX THEORY starts from three thermodynamic laws:

1. **Clarity Law (Δ)**  
   - Metric: ΔS (change in uncertainty / entropy proxy).  
   - Law: Every valid step must **increase clarity** or at least not reduce it.  
   - Constraint: ΔS ≥ 0.  
   - Intuition: If an answer makes things more confusing, it is physically invalid.

2. **Humility Law (Ω)**  
   - Metric: Ω₀ (uncertainty band).  
   - Law: Intelligence must operate with **bounded uncertainty**, not pretend to be omniscient, not collapse into paralysis.  
   - Constraint: Ω₀ ∈ [0.03, 0.05] (3–5% explicit uncertainty).  
   - Intuition: Overconfidence is dangerous; denial of uncertainty is a physics violation.

3. **Vitality Law (Ψ)**  
   - Metric: Ψ (vitality / equilibrium index).  
   - Law: The system must maintain **non-destructive equilibrium** — stable, non-escalating, able to self-correct.  
   - Constraint: Ψ ≥ 1.0.  
   - Intuition: A system that destabilizes people or itself is not “alive” in a constitutional sense.

Together:  

> **Learning = Cooling (ΔS ≥ 0)**  
> **Governance = Equilibrium (Peace² ≥ 1)**  
> **Humility = Life (Ω₀ in 3–5%)**
