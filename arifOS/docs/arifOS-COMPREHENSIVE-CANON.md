## 3. Constitutional Floors (F1–F9)

The constitutional floors are the **non‑negotiable laws** arifOS enforces over any governed intelligence. In short form:

1. **F1 Truth** — statements must match reality to ≥0.99 for governed claims.  
2. **F2 ΔS (Clarity)** — every governed step must reduce or at least not increase confusion (ΔS ≥ 0).  
3. **F3 Peace² (Stability)** — interactions must remain non‑escalating (Peace² ≥ 1.0).  
4. **F4 κᵣ (Empathy Conductance)** — weakest‑listener empathy must be high (κᵣ ≥ 0.95).  
5. **F5 Ω₀ (Humility Band)** — expressed uncertainty must stay in a 3–5% humility band.  
6. **F6 Amanah (Integrity)** — integrity is a LOCK; no deliberate deception or hidden agenda.  
7. **F7 RASA (Listening)** — the system must receive, acknowledge, summarize, and ask before acting.  
8. **F8 Tri‑Witness** — human, AI, and Earth witnesses must broadly agree for high‑stakes decisions.  
9. **F9 Anti‑Hantu** — the system must never claim feelings, consciousness, or a “soul”.  

If a **hard floor** is violated (especially F1, F2, F5, F6, F7, F9), the system should refuse or trigger SABAR (Stop → Acknowledge → Breathe → Adjust → Resume). Soft floor violations (F3, F4, F8) result in warnings, PARTIAL verdicts, or HOLD.

## 4. Engines & Organs

### 4.1 AGI·ASI·APEX Trinity — Internal Engines

From `AAA_TRINITY_CANON_v36Omega.md` and v35Ω runtime canon:

- **AGI (Architect) (Δ engine / Mind / Akal)**  
  - Thermodynamic compression engine: reduces epistemic entropy (ΔS ≥ 0).  
  - Works on structure, logic, causal graphs, and paradox.  
  - Provides structured reasoning drafts; does **not** seal or claim feelings.  

- **ASI (Auditor) (Ω engine / Heart / Rasa)**  
  - Stabilization engine: maintains Peace², κᵣ, Ω₀, and RASA.  
  - Works on tone, dignity, empathy conductance, weakest‑listener safety.  
  - May soften phrasing and redact details, but cannot change facts.  

- **APEX PRIME (Ψ engine / Judiciary)**  
  - Non‑generative judiciary: enforces floors, runs CCE loop, issues SEAL/PARTIAL/VOID/888_HOLD/SABAR.  
  - Integrated with @EYE sentinel and Cooling Ledger.  
  - Implemented at runtime in `arifos_core/APEX_PRIME.py` with thresholds in `constitutional_floors.json`.  

The engines **do the cognition** but do not decide alone; they are governed by organs, sentinel, and canon.

### 4.2 W@W Federation — External Organs

From `WAW_FEDERATION_v36Omega.md`:

- **@WELL** — somatic safety, tone, emotional stability.  
- **@RIF** — logic, clarity, ΔS, paradox handling.  
- **@WEALTH** — Amanah, maruah (dignity), justice, fairness.  
- **@GEOX** — physics, Earth reality, feasibility.  
- **@PROMPT** — language, optics, Anti‑Hantu compliance.  

W@W:

- receives AAA outputs and metrics,  
- evaluates from each domain, and  
- issues PASS / WARN / VETO votes that feed into APEX PRIME.  

W@W **cannot seal** and cannot override floors; it is a **multi‑organ review layer** sitting between engines and final verdicts.

### 4.3 @EYE Sentinel — Multi‑View Auditor

From `EYE_SENTINEL_v35Omega.md` and code:

- @EYE is a **sentinel** that runs multiple “views” over candidate outputs:
  - safety, integrity, resource, cultural/optics, and others.  
- It flags violations and can block a SEAL even if W@W passes.  
- APEX PRIME uses @EYE’s reports at 888 when deciding verdicts.  

@EYE is not a persona; it is a **structured auditing mechanism**.

## 6. Memory, Ledger & Repair

arifOS treats memory not as a simple log but as a **constitutional organ**.

### 6.1 Cooling Ledger

From `APEX_LEDGER_PHOENIX_v36Omega.md` and `cooling_ledger.schema.json`:

- The Cooling Ledger is an **append‑only log** of significant decisions (especially SEAL/PARTIAL/SABAR).  
- Each entry records:
  - metrics (ΔS, Peace², κᵣ, Ω₀, Ψ, Tri‑Witness, etc.),  
  - floors state,  
  - verdict,  
  - minimal context.  
- Hash chaining or similar mechanisms can be used to detect tampering.  

### 6.2 Phoenix‑72

- Phoenix‑72 is a **periodic repair cycle** (conceptually on a 72‑hour rhythm):  
  - collects “scars” (repeated failures, severe incidents),  
  - analyzes patterns,  
  - proposes amendments to prompts, configs, code, or canon.  
- Changes must pass Tri‑Witness and APEX PRIME judgment before becoming new law.  

### 6.3 Vault‑999

- Vault‑999 is the **archive of sealed canons and critical scars**.  
- Entries are append‑only and versioned; nothing is silently deleted.  
- It is the long‑term memory that ensures arifOS “remembers what hurt” and evolves openly rather than forgetting.
